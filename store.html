Shipments

Use crystals or credits to purchase gear and character shards. The four gear items at the bottom are very cheap and you should purchase these every chance you get to fulfill your daily challenge requirement and to stockpile gear that you may need in the future. If you do well in arena, you'll have free crystals to spend, otherwise you'll have to purchase crystals with real money.

If you want to spend your crystals wisely, its important to understand that some items are a good value, and other items are a bad value. For example, a full "Mk 3 Carbanti Sensor Array" costs 1400 crystals. With those 1400 crystal you could do 28 refreshes of your Battle Energy which is 3360 energy. If you spend all of that energy farming nodes 6-G-Dark, 7-G-Light, and 8-F-Light you'll get between 70 and 90 "Mk 3 Carbanti Sensor Array Salvage" (It only takes 50 to craft a full array) as well as hundreds of other gear items that you probably need. So, for this gear item, it's a better deal to spend your crystals on refreshes and avoid purchasing it directly from the store. On the other hand, you can purchase a full "Mk 8 Biotech Implant Prototype" for only 750 crystals. With these 750 crystals you could refresh your energy 15 times for 1800 energy. Use this to farm node 9-E-Dark and you'll only get 40 of the Biotech Implant Components (you need 50), so in this case it's a better use of crystals to buy it from the store.

Cantina Store

Shards for various toons as well as Enhancement Droids, Ability Materials and Ship Building Materials. The best thing to spend your Cantina Credits on here is toons that you want, or Ship Building Materials if you are working on ships.

Squad Arena Store

The only place to get some character shards. If you get them all, you can also exchange your Arena Tokens for Credits or Prestige.

Galactic War Store

A place to purchase ship blueprints and character shards. Make 3 purchases per day if you can complete Galactic War.

Guild Store

The only place to get certain character shards. Earn Guild Currency by doing raids and daily challenges. You can also purchase certain rare gear items here.

Fleet Store

This is the only place to purchase Zeta Materials as well as certain rare ship blueprints and character shards. Some gear can also be purchased here.

Shard Shop

Excess character shards get converted into Shard Currency which you can then spend to buy other characters, or some raid level gear.
